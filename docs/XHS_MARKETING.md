**设计意图**：一个诱人的“开始测试”按钮或神秘礼盒，激发点击欲。
**Prompt (推荐 Midjourney)**:
> **English**: A cute 3D rendered main visual for a viral mobile app. In the center, a large glossy button that says "AI TEST" in bold 3D letters. Surrounding floating elements: question marks, percentage symbols %, and lightning bolts. Background is a soft gradient of purple and pink. Pixar style 3D, extremely cute, high quality, trending on ArtStation. --ar 3:4

### 图2：详情页 - 趣味分析雷达 (Analysis)
**设计意图**：展示“分析中”的状态，用雷达图代替人脸，安全又直观。
**Prompt**:
> **English**: A 3D glass interface card showing a radar chart analysis. The radar chart has 5 axes labeled with abstract dots. A progress bar at the bottom says "Loading 99%". Vibrant neon colors, frosted glass effect. Clean and modern UI. --ar 3:4
> **中文描述**：展示雷达图分析的3D玻璃质感界面卡片。雷达图有5个轴，标记着抽象也是点。底部进度条显示“Loading 99%”。充满活力的霓虹色，磨砂玻璃效果。干净现代的UI。

### 图3：详情页 - 极简三步 (Steps)
**设计意图**：傻瓜式操作流程。
**Prompt**:
> **English**: 3D icons floating vertically. 1. A ticket (Activation Code). 2. A cloud with an arrow (Upload). 3. A star icon (Result). Pastel background, soft lighting, 3D clay render style. --ar 3:4

### 图4：详情页 - 必读贴士 (Tips)
**设计意图**：温馨提示，非警告。
**Prompt**:
> **English**: A cute 3D notification bubble. Inside text layout implies "Important Notice". Soft red and white colors. Smooth rounded corners. 3D render. --ar 3:4
> **中文描述**：可爱的3D通知气泡。里面的文字布局暗示“重要通知”。柔和的红白配色。光滑的圆角。3D渲染。
